One Pot Lentil Pasta

Description

Easy one pot lentil pasta recipe is a satisfying, comforting vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of red lentils
  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 10 chery tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 carrot
  • 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. Sauté the onion in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil and add the diced zucchini and carrot.
  2. Sauté for a little longer and add the lentils, pasta and tomatoes.
  3. Add water and cover the pan.
  4. Wait 20 minutes and in the final minutes, uncover the pan so the water evaporates.
